The Event

Demystifying Web3 Communities

This was in-person event hosted in Bangalore. It was the first offline event after a long time by Community Folks.

Details

Planned and organized and hosted the first community-centric Web3 meetup in Bangalore, featuring a panel of established founders and community builders from both the Web2 and Web3 ecosystems.

The purpose of the event was to bring founders, and community builders from both Web 2 and Web 3 ecosystem under one roof. The goal was to build understanding between the two. So, that the founders know what to expect from the community, how to align their goals to it and how to make the best of it. And, the community builders know how to serve the business purpose with the community efforts in Web3.

  • The target audience was Community Builders and Founders
  • We got 200+ registrations, even after being an invite only event
  • 100+ attendees
  • A panel with founders of Redbus, ProductFolks and community builders from Web3
  • We were able to help attendees undesand the basics of he Web3 ecosystem and how are communities different there
