Chronicles of Community Builders

Chronicles of Community Builders is a series of talks where every month, we are highlighting stories of community leaders who are nailing it in the global Community Industry.

A monthly series of talks highlighting successful community leaders in the global Community Industry, with member-hosted events that fostered community-led engagement.

This was a Q&A based series, where, our members got to ask their questions to the guest speaker.

  • 70+ attendees per episode
  • Reach of 10k+ with before and after event posts from Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn
  • Members got to learn from the experience of the experts
  • Case studies and blogs prepared from the learnings of the event
