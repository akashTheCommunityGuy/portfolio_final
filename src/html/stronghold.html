The Organization

Stronghold

Stronghold is the community I built when I was a regular and active World of Warcraft(WOW) player.

Details

I built this community as a gamer and an unofficial community builder. We called it a guild.
The idea to build this community came to me when I had just started playing WOW. I connected with a few other players while exploring the open world. I proposed a small group where we share achievements, items, form raid groups and help each other.
That idea evolved slowly into a thriving community of 1000+ members, connecting over chat and voice. Doing dungeons and raids together. Crafting items for each other.

I never kept a tab on analytics, so I don't have any number to share here. However, I am still connected with a few of the initial members from the community. We call each other occasionally and talk about life and everything else. And I find that to be a better achievement than anything else.
